Consumer Digital Services

Welcome to our comprehensive guide meant to help unravel the complexities of consumer digital services. Digital services are a broad category of services that are delivered to consumers electronically.

These include things like:

  • Internet Service Providers (ISP) (cable, fiber, fixed e.g. Spectrum, Fios, AT&T, etc. )
  • Television (standard cable and satellite channels e.g. Comcast, DirecTV, etc.)
  • Mobile Services (voice, text, data via Verizon, AT&T, T-Mobile, etc.)
  • Streaming Media Services
    • Music streaming (Spotify, YouTube Music, iTunes, etc.)
    • Video streaming (Netflix, Hulu, Max, Prime Video, etc.)
  • Security camera cloud storage (Nest, Wyze, Ring, etc.)
